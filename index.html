Manifesto

The internet broke the relationship between creators and audiences. Algorithms extracted attention. Advertisers extracted data. Platforms extracted value. Everyone got paid — except the people who actually created.

"Give as much as you think it's worth."

Value for value is not a business model. It is a covenant — a commitment to direct, honest exchange between human beings. No middlemen. No paywalls. No surveillance. Just the timeless logic of reciprocity, now running on money that cannot be censored.

V4V is what happens when that covenant meets a vision board. A public ledger of what we're building — and an open invitation to build it with us.
